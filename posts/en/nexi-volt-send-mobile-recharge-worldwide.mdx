## What Is Global Mobile Recharge?

A mobile recharge, also known as a top-up or airtime transfer, is the process of adding prepaid credit to a mobile phone account. In many countries across Asia, Latin America, and Africa, the majority of mobile phone users are on prepaid plans. They buy credit as needed to make calls, send texts, and use mobile data.

Global mobile recharge services allow you to purchase that credit remotely and send it directly to a phone number in another country. For the millions of immigrants living in the United States, this is one of the most practical ways to support family members back home.

That is exactly what Nexi Volt is designed to do.

## How Nexi Volt Works

Nexi Volt is Nexitel's global recharge platform. It allows you to send mobile credit to phones in countries around the world directly from your smartphone or computer. The process takes just a few steps:

1. **Select the country and carrier** where your recipient's phone is registered.
2. **Enter the phone number** you want to recharge.
3. **Choose the recharge amount** from available denominations.
4. **Complete the payment** securely through the Nexi Volt platform.
5. **The credit is delivered instantly** to the recipient's phone.

There are no apps to install on the recipient's end. They do not need internet access or a smartphone. The credit simply appears in their prepaid balance, ready to use immediately.

## Why Immigrants Rely on Mobile Recharge Services

For immigrant communities in the US, staying connected with family back home is a daily priority. While money transfers through services like Western Union and Remitly serve an important purpose, they are not always the right tool for every situation.

### When Your Family Needs Phone Credit, Not Cash

Sometimes the most immediate need is not rent money or groceries. It is simply the ability to make a phone call, access WhatsApp, or use mobile data. Sending a five or ten dollar recharge lets your family member stay connected to you and to their local community without having to visit a store or wait for a bank transfer.

### Faster Than Traditional Remittance

Wire transfers can take one to three business days. Mobile recharges through Nexi Volt arrive in seconds. When your mother's phone runs out of credit and she cannot call you back, speed matters.

### More Affordable Than You Think

Nexi Volt offers competitive exchange rates and low transaction fees. In many cases, the cost of sending a recharge is significantly less than the fees charged by traditional money transfer services for small amounts.
