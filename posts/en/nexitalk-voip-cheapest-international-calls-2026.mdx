## What Is NexiTalk and How Does It Work?

NexiTalk is a VoIP calling service that routes your voice calls over the internet instead of traditional phone lines. This means you can make and receive calls from anywhere you have a WiFi or mobile data connection. There is no special hardware required. NexiTalk works on your smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer.

Unlike basic messaging apps that only let you call other users on the same platform, NexiTalk lets you dial real phone numbers in over 50 countries. Your contacts do not need to download any app or have internet access. They simply answer their regular phone.

## How NexiTalk Compares to WhatsApp, Skype, and Other Apps

Free calling apps like WhatsApp and Skype are popular, but they come with significant limitations that NexiTalk addresses.

### The Other-User Problem

WhatsApp and FaceTime only work when both parties have the app installed and an active internet connection. If your grandmother in a rural area uses a basic phone, these apps are useless. NexiTalk dials real phone numbers, so it works no matter what device your contact uses.

### Call Quality and Reliability

Free apps route calls through shared servers with no quality-of-service guarantees. During peak hours, you often experience dropped calls, lag, and muffled audio. NexiTalk uses dedicated VoIP infrastructure optimized for voice traffic, delivering consistent HD quality even during high-traffic periods.
